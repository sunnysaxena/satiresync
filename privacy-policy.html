Privacy Policy for SatireSync

Effective Date: April 12, 2025

Welcome to SatireSync. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

SatireSync does not collect or store any personal data from users. We use public news and sports headlines to generate humorous content.

However, when using our app in connection with Facebook or Instagram, we may access:

  • Public Instagram profile information (e.g., Page ID, post permissions)
  • Page media publishing access via the Instagram Graph API

This access is read-only or publish-only and limited to performing automation on your behalf.

2. How We Use the Information

We use authorized access to:

  • Generate AI-based meme captions from news sources
  • Create memes and automatically post them to your linked Instagram page

We do not:

  • Sell or share your data
  • Store any sensitive user information
  • Send messages or perform actions outside the described functionality

3. Third-Party Services

SatireSync uses the following services:

  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram Graph API) to post content
  • Open-source LLMs (e.g., Ollama) for generating captions
  • Public news APIs to fetch trending headlines

4. Data Security

We do not store any user data on our servers. All API tokens and access credentials are securely stored locally or on the user's system during use and never transmitted to third parties.

By using SatireSync, you consent to this privacy policy and agree to its terms.
